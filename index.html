Synclist

For sellers and dropshippers. With powerful API integration.

Sync stocks

Sync stock figures from your eBay and Etsy stores, show them in one place in one listing. Optionally combine if you sell similar products on multiple platforms.

Sync and manage orders

Your store orders are synced in the background so when you view the orders listing you see orders from all your selling platforms together. You can arrange with specific criteria like price range, titular similarity, order rages, shipping location.

Combine similar orders

You can combine orders that will deliver to a same address. Orders are synced in background and similar looking orders are suggested in the orders listing panel. This removes a great hassle in shipping.

Verify shipping address

You can verify shipping address, get suggestions or errors on any address, free for all the 51 states.

Set tracking code

You can set tracking code to any of your store from one place. Just put the order identifier number or find the order by powerful search, and put the tracking code, and its done.

Design template and generate shipping label

Powerful template designer to design your shipping label template. Select an order, select a label template and hit generate. With our driver for windows machines, you can directly print it too.
